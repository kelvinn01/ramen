Delicious Ramen Recipes

Try these mouth-watering ramen dishes and bring authentic flavors to your kitchen!

Pork Ramen

Ingredients:

  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 2 cups pork broth
  • 100g sliced pork belly
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Chopped green onions
  • Nori (seaweed)

Instructions:

  1. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Heat pork broth in a pot and add soy sauce and sesame oil.
  3. Place noodles in a bowl and pour the hot broth over them.
  4. Top with sliced pork belly, boiled egg, green onions, and nori.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Spicy Chicken Ramen

Ingredients:

  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 150g shredded chicken
  • 1 tbsp chili paste (e.g., gochujang)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic (minced)
  • 1 boiled egg
  • Chopped green onions
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Heat chicken broth in a pot and mix in chili paste, soy sauce, and garlic.
  3. Add shredded chicken to the broth and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Place noodles in a bowl and pour the spicy chicken broth over them.
  5. Top with boiled egg, green onions, and sesame seeds.
  6. Serve and enjoy the heat!

Vegan Ramen

Ingredients:

  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., mushrooms, spinach, carrots)
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Tofu cubes (optional)
  • Chopped green onions
  • Nori (seaweed)

Instructions:

  1. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Heat vegetable broth in a pot and mix in miso paste, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and simmer until tender.
  4. Place noodles in a bowl and pour the vegetable broth over them.
  5. Top with tofu cubes, green onions, and nori.
  6. Enjoy this wholesome vegan option!
